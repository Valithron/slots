How to play

Every spin activates five lines: the three horizontal rows and both diagonals. The total bet is five times the line bet.

  • Three matching portraits on a line pay according to the table.
  • The silver Tree of Life remains Wild and substitutes for any portrait.
  • A Tree that naturally lands in the exact center cell may awaken and fill the middle reel with Wilds.
  • Three natural Trees, one visible on each reel, trigger four Commune Free Spins. Tree Awakening cannot create this trigger.
  • Before new Free Spins begin, choose one of seven allies and confirm. The choice cannot change after the feature starts.
  • Sterling insures losses; Ryan marks one of the first four spins for 2×; Cooper builds Rage after losses; Cydney echoes 45% of the first win.
  • Gabi replays the first weak win below 3× total bet from a win-only pool; Kenly adds 37% to natural Small Wins; Ashley replays the first loss.
  • The same natural Three Trees event during the feature retriggers two spins, up to twenty total awarded spins.
  • Free spins use the triggering total bet. They cost no coins and do not build, consume, or multiply with Fortune.
  • All ally calculations use integer coins and round down. Replays are predetermined and settle only the accepted result.
  • Tree Awakening, Commune combinations, all five paylines, character reactions, and manual reel stopping remain active during free spins.
  • Named combinations trigger when the required three symbols appear in any order across the middle Commune Line.
  • Top and bottom rows, vertical columns, diagonals, and other paylines do not trigger named combinations.
  • Full Commune requires all seven members in the grid and the Tree in the exact center cell.
  • Every paid spin adds Fortune. Natural wins and Commune combinations add more.
  • At 100 Fortune, the next paid spin becomes a Fortune Spin and multiplies the complete wager-generated win by 1.5×, rounded down.
  • A losing Fortune Spin still consumes the charge and begins the next meter cycle.
  • While reels move, press Stop, Space, or Enter to request the next reel stop. Reels always stop left to right.
  • Manual stopping changes presentation timing only. It cannot change the result, payout, features, or odds.
  • Character reactions and ally panels use the current portraits. Missing future variants safely fall back to the base portrait, then the Tree.
  • Nice Wins and larger presentations can be skipped with Spin, Enter, or Space.
  • Your fake coin balance, Fortune Meter, pending result, ally choice, free-spin session, feature rolls, and settings save automatically in this browser.
