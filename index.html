How to play

Every spin activates five lines: the three horizontal rows and both diagonals. The total bet is five times the line bet.

  • Three matching portraits on a line pay according to the table.
  • The silver Tree of Life remains Wild and substitutes for any portrait.
  • A Tree that naturally lands in the exact center cell may awaken and fill the middle reel with Wilds.
  • The middle row is the Commune Line. Named trios require the exact left-to-right order shown below.
  • Full Commune requires all seven members in the grid and the Tree in the exact center cell.
  • Combination bonuses add to ordinary line wins before the final win tier is determined.
  • Nice Wins and larger presentations can be skipped with Spin, Enter, or Space.
  • Your fake coin balance, pending result, feature roll, and settings save automatically in this browser.
